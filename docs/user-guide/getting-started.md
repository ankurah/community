# Getting started

## Signing in

Community uses [idp.to](https://idp.to) for sign-in, with **passkeys** — so
there is no password to remember. A passkey is unlocked by your device (a
fingerprint, face, or screen lock), which is both quicker and safer than a
password.

1. On the welcome screen, click **Sign in with idp.to**.
2. idp.to takes over to confirm it's you. If you're new, it walks you through
   creating a passkey; if you've been here before, you approve with the passkey
   you already have.
3. You're returned to Community, signed in and ready to chat.

You stay signed in across page reloads, so you won't need to repeat this every
visit. If sign-in doesn't complete, the welcome screen shows a short message
explaining why — usually it just needs another try in a moment.

## Your display name

Your name in the top bar is how you appear to everyone. To change it, **click
your name**, type a new one, and press **Enter**. The change is live: it
updates on your messages and in the members list for everyone, straight away.

## Account settings

The **gear** button in the top bar opens your idp.to account, where you manage
the things that belong to your identity rather than to Community — your
passkeys, your recovery email, and your profile. When you're done, idp.to
brings you back to Community.

## Opening Community on another device

The **QR code** button (the small grid icon) shows a code titled *Scan to
connect*. Point your phone's camera at it to open this same page on your phone,
where you can sign in the same way.

## Signing out

The **Sign out** button signs you out of Community and of idp.to together. That
means the next time you sign in, you'll be asked for your passkey again rather
than being let straight back in — which is what you want on a shared or public
computer.
