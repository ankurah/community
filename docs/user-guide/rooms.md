# Rooms

Conversations are organised into **rooms**, listed down the left sidebar. Each
room is a separate space with its own history.

## Switching rooms

Click a room in the sidebar to open it. The web address updates to match, so
you can **bookmark a room or share its link** and land right back in the same
place. If you arrive without a room chosen, Community opens **general** by
default.

Scrolled up into older messages? A **Jump to latest** button appears to whisk
you back to the live end of the conversation.

## Creating a room

Press the **+** at the top of the sidebar, type a name, and press **Enter**.
Anyone can create a room, and you're taken straight into the new one. Press
**Esc** (or leave the name blank) to cancel.

## Unread badges

A room with messages you haven't seen yet shows a small **count badge**. It
counts messages from other people that arrived since you last looked; your own
messages never count as unread. Large counts are shown as **10+**.

Your reading position is remembered **per room and follows you across devices**,
so a room you've caught up with on your laptop won't still look unread on your
phone. A badge clears once you're viewing the latest messages in that room.

## Room topics

The current room's **topic** — a short line describing what the room is for —
appears in the top bar next to the room name. Hover it to read the full text if
it's long.

If you're the room's **creator** or a **moderator**, the topic is editable:
click it, type (up to 200 characters), and press **Enter**. The default rooms
that came with Community have no single creator, so only moderators can set
their topics.
