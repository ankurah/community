# People

## The members list

The **Members** button in the top bar opens the members panel — the full roster
of everyone in the community. Each row shows a person's avatar, display name,
and any role badges. A count at the top tells you how many members there are,
and the list updates live as people join or change their names.

## Roles and badges

Community has three roles:

- **Member** — everyone. Members have no badge; it's simply the baseline.
- **Moderator** — carries a **Moderator** badge and can keep the community tidy
  (see [Moderation](moderation.md)).
- **Admin** — carries an **Admin** badge and has the same moderation abilities.

Roles are **managed by the identity provider**, not from inside the chat. If
your role changes, it takes effect the **next time you sign in** — the panel
notes this at the bottom, so it's never a mystery why a new badge hasn't
appeared yet.

## Profile cards

Click anyone's **avatar or name on a message** to open their profile card. It
shows their avatar, display name, when they were **first seen** in the
community, and their role badges. It's a quick way to place who said something
without leaving the conversation.

## Bans, briefly

Moderators also see a "Banned" badge and a per-member action menu here for
banning and unbanning. Regular members don't see other people's ban status at
all. The full picture is in [Moderation](moderation.md).
