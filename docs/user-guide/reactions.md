# Reactions

React to any message with a quick emoji — a light way to say "yes", "thanks",
"ha", or "noted" without adding to the conversation.

## The reaction set

Community offers a small, fixed set of six reactions:

👍 ❤️ 😂 🎉 😕 👀

A deliberately short list keeps reactions fast and readable — no giant emoji
keyboard to scroll through.

## Adding and removing a reaction

- **To react:** hover a message and click the **⋯** button (or right-click the
  message) to open its actions, then click one of the emoji at the top.
- **To remove your reaction:** click it again. Reacting is a toggle.

Once a message has reactions, they appear as small **chips** just beneath it.
You can also click a chip directly to add or remove that same reaction.

## Reading the chips

Each chip shows the emoji and a **count** — how many people reacted with it. If
you're one of them, your chip is highlighted, so you can tell at a glance which
reactions are yours. Reactions sync live, so counts tick up and down as people
react from anywhere.
